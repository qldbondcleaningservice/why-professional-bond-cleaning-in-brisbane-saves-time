Understanding Bond Cleaning in Brisbane

When you first move into a rental property, your landlord or property manager records its condition in an entry condition report. When you move out, the property must be returned to that same level of cleanliness (allowing for fair wear and tear).

If the property doesn't meet inspection standards, part of your bond may be withheld to cover cleaning costs. With bonds often worth thousands of dollars, this isn't something tenants can afford to risk.
